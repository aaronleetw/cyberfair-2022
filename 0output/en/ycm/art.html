YCM Public Artworks


The Colorful Clouds Liu Chen-chou, 2000

Every cloud has various shapes, sizes, and colors, it creates a peaceful and lively scene

The Arch of Millennium Chiu Huan-tang, 2000

Combines the rainbow and the arch, an interesting spatial layers are shown above the lake.

Life in Ceramics Jeff Wu, 2009

The ceramic plates and cups set randomly on the grassland which creates a leisure atmosphere.

Walking Clay Kung Wen-yi, 2009

This work explores the ideas of “walking in clay, in the form of clay,” merging the idea that clays and part of human life and can't disappear.

A King’s Staff - 1804 Shih Hsuan-yu, 2009

The ceramics industry in Yingge began in 1804 CE. More than 200 years later, it has become Taiwan’s most essential hub of ceramic products. This exceedingly big equipment represents how big Yingge is in ceramic history.

The Legend of the Crescent Moon Gate
Deng Hui-fen, 1997

This artwork is made up of 9 pieces of ceramics, after coloring and editing, creates the spatial layer of environment and city.
